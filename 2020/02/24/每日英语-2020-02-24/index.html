每日英语-2020-02-24

2020-02-24 每日英语

文章目录

  1. 今日语句

今日语句

  1. I’m scard of the bear; don’t let it get near me!
    我怕熊，别让他靠近我！

  2. I bought a red chair.
    我买了一把红色椅子

  3. He always wears a shirt and tie to work.
    他去工作时总是穿衬衫打领带

  4. I drink the water because I’m thirsty.
    我口渴所以喝水

  5. My sister and I water the plants.
    姐姐和我在给植物浇水

  6. Milk is a tasty and healthy drink.
    牛奶是一种可口又健康的饮品

  7. Would you like a cup of tea?
    你要来杯茶么？

  8. I want to drink orange juice!
    我好想喝橘子汁啊！

  9. I want to find a job from the newspaper.
    我想要从报纸上找一份工作

  10. I’m very hungry,so i can eat a lot.
    我很饿，因此我可以吃很多.

  11. Fish have very simple lives.
    鱼过着简单的生活

  12. These chickens look so different.
    这些鸡长得很不一样

  13. I’ll have some eggs for breakfast.
    我早餐会吃一些鸡蛋

  14. I’ll cook rice for dinner.
    晚餐我做米饭

  15. The boy is saying sorry to his girlfriend.
    男孩在给他女朋友道歉

  16. She says,”Sure! It’s OK!”
    她说：“当然了！没问题！”

  17. I like the green doll.
    我喜欢这个绿色的玩偶

  18. My bed is so comfortable.
    我的床相当舒服

  19. The box of books is really heavy.
    这一箱书可真沉啊

  20. She will write down her menu on the blackboard.
    她会把她的菜单写在这块黑板上

  21. The black car looks cool and expensive.
    那辆黑色的车看起来气派豪华

  22. The train is about to travel through the building.
    列车即将穿楼而过

  23. Kick the ball into the net.
    把球踢进网

  24. I like to travel by plane.
    我喜欢乘坐飞机旅行

  25. Who is the girl standing behind the boy?
    站在那个男孩后面的女孩是谁？

  26. Where does the door lead to?
    这个门通向哪里？

  27. There is a ball near the hole.
    那个球在洞口附近

  28. On holidays,fireworks light up the sky.
    在节日，烟火照亮填空

  29. The cat is hiding under the sofa.
    那只猫躲在沙发下面

  30. All of my textbooks are on my desk.
    我所有的课本都在我的书桌上

  31. There is an empty desk right here.
    这里有一张空桌子
